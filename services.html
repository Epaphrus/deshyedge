FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common Questions

Find answers to the most frequently asked questions about our consulting services and engagement process.

DeshyEdge Consulting specializes in several key industries including healthcare, finance, manufacturing, and technology. Our consultants have deep domain expertise in these sectors, allowing us to provide tailored solutions that address industry-specific challenges and opportunities.

Project timelines vary based on scope, complexity, and organizational readiness. Small-scale projects might take 4-8 weeks, while comprehensive transformations can span 6-12 months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline based on your specific needs and objectives.

Our consulting approach is collaborative and results-driven. We begin with a thorough assessment of your current state, work closely with your team to develop tailored solutions, implement changes with careful change management, and measure outcomes against defined KPIs. We emphasize knowledge transfer to ensure your team can sustain improvements long after our engagement ends.

Our pricing is customized based on the scope, complexity, and duration of each project. We offer flexible engagement models including project-based, retainer, and value-based pricing. During our initial consultation, we'll discuss your specific needs and provide a transparent pricing proposal that aligns with your budget and expected outcomes. We believe in delivering value that exceeds our fees.

We establish clear, measurable KPIs at the beginning of each engagement, aligned with your business objectives. These might include financial metrics (ROI, cost savings), operational metrics (efficiency, productivity), or strategic outcomes (market share, customer satisfaction). We provide regular progress reports and a comprehensive final assessment to demonstrate the value delivered.

DeshyEdge differentiates itself through our deep industry expertise, practical implementation focus, and commitment to sustainable results. We don't just provide recommendationswe partner with you to implement solutions and build internal capabilities. Our consultants bring both global best practices and local market understanding, offering a unique blend of strategic insight and practical execution.
